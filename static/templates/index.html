Frequently Asked Questions


What is exactly the country code?

The country code will depend on the origin country of your mobile phone's plan. Here you can find a list of some country codes:
-USA: 1
-Canada: 1
-UK: 44
-France: 33
-Australia: 61
-Spain: 34
-Germany: 49
-Italy: 39


What is exactly the pre-filled message?

This is the pre-filled message it would appear on your customer's phone while initiating the chat with you.


Will this app work on a desktop?

The app will work on desktops, however the customer should use Whatsapp web.
